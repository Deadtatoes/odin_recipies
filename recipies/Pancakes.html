Recipie for Pancakes



Ingredients For Pancakes

  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Nutmeg
  • Vanilla Essence

Process In Making Pancakes

  1. Whisking :Whisk dry ingredients together

  2. Melting ingredients :Melt buttter and warm milk[microwave].

  3. Whisking wet ingredients :Whisk the rest of the ingredients after washing them with water.

  4. Combining ingredients :Here we mix both the dry ingredients and the wet ingredients together.

  5. Frying :the mixed ingredients are whisked one last time and then the paste is heated with a frying pan.

  6. Enjoy.
