Recipie for Cheesecake

Ingredients for cheesecake

  • Block cream cheese
  • Sugar
  • Sour cream
  • Flavor
  • Eggs
  • Milk


Recipie For Cheesecake

  1. Setting up the pie pan : Set up the pie pan on the baking sheet if desired. This is to make transportation easier.

  2. Making the crust :Stir all the crust ingredients thoroughly until its evenly mixed.

  3. Pressing the crumbs :Press the crumbs into the bottom and up the sides of the pie pan, making a thick layer at the bottom than the sides.

  4. Baking :Put the cheesecake in the oven and bake it for 20mins. Then add the crust shield, leaving it to bake fo an extra 10 mins.

  5. Cooling :Take the cheesecake out of the oven and leave it to cool.

  6. Enjoy :Serve cheesecake in wedges with fillings and fruits if desired.
