Guacamole Recipie


Ingredients For Guacamole
  • Avocado
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Coriander
  • Chili pepper
  • Jalapeno pepper
  • Garlic
  • Lime
  • Salt
  • Red onion

Steps In Making Guacamole

  1. Prepare avocados:first, cut the avocados into halves and remove the seed. Place them into a large bowl.

  2. Mash avocado:Using a pastry cutter or fork cut into avocados and keep cutting until it’s about the texture you want (I like mine like mine to have a bit chunky with texture).

  3. Add in remaining ingredients:next add in tomatoes, red onion, cilantro, garlic, jalapeño, salt, pepper and lime juice.

  4. Toss :toss mixture together and serve with tortilla chips and all your favorite Mexican food!

  5. Enjoy
